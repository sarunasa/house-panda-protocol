# World Models Expansion Roadmap

*From LLMs to Financial World Models — Research Direction*

---

## Current State: LLMs as Management Layer

LLM-based stack: Claude/GPT analyze data, make decisions, generate reports. Text-in, text-out. Works today.

But market making is a **dynamics problem** — order books, liquidity flows, price impact, volatility regimes. Closer to physics simulations than text prediction.

---

## Where World Models Connect to House Panda

### 1. Bonding Curve Simulation
The bonding curve is deterministic physics — supply in, price out. World model simulates millions of MM scenarios, stress-tests curve, predicts Inference Bank needs.

### 2. MM Performance Evaluation
Simulate counterfactual: what would have happened without this MM's activity? Sophisticated contribution pricing.

### 3. Default Mechanism Calibration
Optimize curve-shift magnitude in simulation rather than learning from real defaults.

### 4. Synthetic Training Data
Generate realistic market conditions for training agents on scenarios that haven't happened yet.
