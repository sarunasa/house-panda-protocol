## The Perception Gap

Asia produces huge number of major token projects, but perception gap exists because industry's marketing/narrative infrastructure is English-language and US-centric.

## Major Asian-Origin Tokens

**Singapore:** CRO, VeChain, Zilliqa, Enjin, Kyber Network  
**China/HK:** NEO, Conflux, Tron, BNB (3rd largest by market cap)  
**Korea:** ICON, Klaytn (Kakao-backed), Terra/LUNA (pre-collapse)  
**Japan:** Astar, IOST, Jasmy (ex-Sony), Soramitsu  
**SE Asia:** Axie Infinity (Vietnam)

## Why Perception Gap Exists

1. Singapore incorporation ≠ Singapore identity — projects market as "global"
2. China's 2021 ban scattered teams to Singapore, Dubai, Caymans
3. US VC dominates English-language funding narratives
4. Biggest Asian successes were biggest disasters (Terra, FTX, Mt. Gox)

**What exists:** Stale L1s from 2017-18, peaked gaming tokens, exchange tokens with baggage
